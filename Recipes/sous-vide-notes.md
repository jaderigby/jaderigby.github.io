# Sous Vide Cooking #

## Steak ##
	- frozen/semi frozen
		- 1hr 45min at 126f = medium well
		- 1hr 45min at 123f = medium
		- 1hr 45min at 121f = medium rare

## Pork Chops ##
	- 1hr at 136f - 137f
	- When Frozen:
		- 1 & 1/2 hrs at 136f - 137f

## Halibut ##
	- 40min at 121f

## Chicken Breast ##
	- 1hr 45min at 136f

## Sliders ##
	- 1hr at 133f

## Shrimp Kabob (or just shrimp) ##
	- 15min at 148f
